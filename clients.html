Clients

Who we serve

From one-aircraft private owners to multi-aircraft schools, CloudBaseGA runs flight logging and operations across general aviation, with subscriptions in GBP, EUR and USD.

Flying schools

Bookings, student records, tech logs and billing in one place, with accurate hours feeding training records and invoices automatically.

Clubs & groups

Fair, accurate billing between members, a shared calendar everyone can use, and a tech log that keeps the group honest, without a volunteer doing a day of admin.

Private owners

Every flight recorded without touching a thing: hours, dates and locations logged automatically for maintenance, insurance and your own logbook.

Maintenance engineers

Accurate airframe and engine hours in real time, defect reports as they happen, and check forecasts you can plan a workshop around.

Airfields

Automatic movement logging for based and visiting AutoLog-equipped aircraft, giving accurate records without a clipboard at the window.

The wider GA community

Whether you fly one aircraft or one hundred, if paper records are slowing you down, CloudBaseGA was built for you.
