Confirmation & delivery

We confirm your order, dispatch the AutoLog kit and email your account login details. There's no upfront charge for the unit or its delivery, but for aircraft based outside the UK we first invoice a refundable €300 / $300 deposit per AutoLog.

The AutoLog is rented: return the equipment and any deposit is refunded, less outstanding charges. Installation costs and postage are not refundable.
