How these numbers are calculated

Cost: our standard monthly rate per aircraft plus the per-flight rate, at your fleet size and flying rate, using whichever activity option is cheaper (rotary-wing rates are double). Value recovered: your flights per month × timing error × (hire rate + maintenance cost) per minute, since each minute of error is unbilled hire time and early maintenance. Net saving: value recovered minus cost. Admin hours assume about 6 minutes of logging and transcribing per flight. Estimates, not a quote.

Common questions

The fine print, in plain English

Is there a minimum commitment?

No. There are no fixed-term contracts and no minimum commitment. The equipment is rented as part of the subscription, and returning it is all that's needed to cease the service.

What are the setup, delivery and installation costs?

There's no upfront charge for the AutoLog or its delivery. Installation is arranged and paid for by you (we don't cover fitting costs), and postage is not refundable. Aircraft based outside the UK need a €300 / $300 deposit per AutoLog, which is returned when the equipment is.

How do the low- and high-activity options work?

You choose, per aircraft; it's entirely optional. High-activity pricing has a higher monthly cost but works out better value once an aircraft flies more than roughly 200 hours a year; low-activity pricing suits aircraft that fly less. A working school aircraft and a group tourer on the same fleet can each be on the option that suits them, and you can select whichever you prefer.

I'm a private owner, or I only want the API. What do I pay?

Single-pilot private owners, and API users who aren't looking for the full platform, get our "best of both" rate: the lower monthly charge paired with the lower per-flight charge, rather than choosing between the low- and high-activity options. API subscriptions, where flight records feed a third-party platform instead of ours, generally cost less than the full platform. Get in touch for a figure.

Which currencies do you bill in?

GBP (£), EUR (€) or USD ($), depending on your market. UK customers pay by Direct Debit; we'll confirm payment arrangements for other regions when you order.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes. We usually offer a volume discount for fleets of 10 aircraft or more. Get in touch to discuss terms.
